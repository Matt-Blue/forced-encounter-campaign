About Matthew

Computer science major at the University of Florida pursuing a career in software development.

Spanish minor with full professional communication ability.


Highly organized and independent; able to effectively coordinate tasks and accomplish projects with timeliness and creativity.

Enjoys self-teaching and has the patience necessary to teach those around him, creating a symbiotic culture of cooperation.

With a high-energy and infectious enthusiasm for technology, he is low-maintenance, motivated and productive.

Although programming can be frustrating at times, he remains able to adapt to changing priorities and maintain a positive attitude and strong work ethic.

Has a particular strength in bridging cultural gaps after being involved in a multicultural fraternity and multiple cultural organizations on UF campus.

Hi! My name is Matthew Bluestein
Most people call me Matt Blue

Skiing is one of my greatest passions. The adrenaline rush of flying down the mountain at speeds you aren’t even allowed to drive at, or jumping off cliffs that were taller than I cannot be found elsewhere. This being said, I found a much more safe option to get my thrill; programming. Although it can never replace skiing, I get a huge thrill every time a piece of functionality falls into place. To see my latest thrill-rides click here.

My mother, a graduate from Indiana University with a Bachelor of Arts degree, did not hesitate to get my into art as soon as possible. Since I was little I’ve experimented with every mode of art from painting to sculpting to photography, but I enjoy colored pencils the most. This is because they allow me to create precise detail while giving me complete creative control. To see my best artwork, click here.

I’ve been studying Spanish for over 9 years now. It started as a useful skill learned in school, and has developed into a great appreciation for not only the language, but also the culture. To learn more about my journey through the Spanish language, click here.

One element of my personality that truly defines me is my selflessness. I recycle religiously, perform road cleanups every month, and am cutting down on the amount of meat that I consume. On top of this, I have tried to make my presence in the community one of a positive nature. To see my other community service efforts, click here.
